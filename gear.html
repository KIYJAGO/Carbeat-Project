Our Gear

Every great shot begins with the right tools and we've carefully chosen each piece of gear to bring your moments to life. From high-resolution cameras to versatile lenses and reliable lighting, every item in our kit is selected for its ability to capture detail, color, and emotion with precision.

We believe that equipment is more than just technology; it's a trusted partner in storytelling. That's why we work with industry-leading tools like the Sony A7III for cinematic videography and the Sony A7R for breathtaking photography. Each has its own strengths, allowing us to adapt to any style whether it's a warm, romantic wedding or a bold, modern portrait session.

Just like every person has a unique story, every piece of our gear plays a unique role in telling it. In our hands, these tools aren't just cameras and lenses they're the bridge between your memories and timeless images

Behind the Lens

Photography

Sony A7R

Lightweight and detail-driven the Sony A7R is our choice for high-resolution, nuanced still imagery.

  • Equipped with a 36.4 MP full-frame CMOS sensor without an optical low-pass filter, delivering incredibly sharp imagery and exceptional detail
  • Weighing just 465 g (with batteries), making it highly portable and perfect for on-location shoots
  • Despite lacking IBIS, its compact size and ergonomic design make handheld captures stable and comfortable even during long sessions

Every frame from portraits to landscapes becomes a textured memory, thanks to the sheer resolution and clarity this camera brings.

Sony A7iii

Compact yet powerful the Sony A7III is our workhorse for cinematic video storytelling.

  • 4K recording up to 30 fps for crisp, cinematic quality
  • Full HD 120 fps for smooth slow-motion
  • 5-axis in-body image stabilization (IBIS)
  • Weather-sealed magnesium alloy body

It's the perfect blend of performance and reliability allowing us to capture cinematic-quality video while staying agile on the move.

We offer a wide range of lens options tailored to match each client's specific needs, from wide landscapes to intimate portraits and cinematic close ups.
